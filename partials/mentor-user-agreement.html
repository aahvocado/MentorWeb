BME Mentorship Program Mentor User Agreement

I understand this program requires at the very least 3-5 hours of my time each month for a year and pledge to commit my time, energy, and expertise to assist my mentees in developing and achieving their individual goals.

I realize that this is a vertical mentorship program that links alumni to upperclassmen to freshmen. As such, in addition to acting as a mentor to freshmen, I am expected to join the student alumni association (SAA) and complete the process for obtaining an alumni mentor by participating in the Mentor Jackets program.

I commit to building a mutual relationship and to entering the world of someone else, to hear about dreams and challenges, to share my own stories, and to respect the differences between us.

I understand the BME Mentorship Program staff pairs student mentees with the most suitable mentors using a variety of factors and that the freshmen I am paired with depends on the cumulative pool of mentors and freshmen who register for the program.

I understand that the academic and professional developmental needs otf the students are the central focus in the pairing process and that BME Mentorship Program administrators endeavor to create pairs with similar education and professional interests.

I understand the quality and scope of any mentoring partnership is largely determined by the commitment and quality of communication between myself and any student mentee I am paired with.

I understand that the BME Mentorship Program's primary form of communication will be through its T-square site which I agree to join immediately.

I understand that the BME MEntoring Program's T-square site will use my official Georgia Tech e-mail for communications and I will check my email regularly to maintain communication with the program and my mentees.

I understand that my involvement in the BME Mentorship Program is at the discretion of the BME Mentorship Program administrators and that the administrators reserve the right to terminate my participation at any time.

If at any point, I am no longer able to, or no longer want to participate in the program, I promise to notify my student mentees and BME Mentorship Program administrators about my situation as soon as possibile so they can find another mentor for my students.

I understand that the BME Mentorship Program administrators are available to help me and provide resources to enhance the mentoring experience and I can contact them at any time at joe.ledoux@gme.gatech.edu.

I understand that trust is fundamental to building sound relationships with and between mentors and mentees and, thus, agree to ethically protect the confidentiality of the students in charge by avoiding talking about them to other students.

I will notify Dr. Le Doux, Dr. Fasse, Kim Paige, or Paul Fincannon confidentially if I perceive that one of the mentees needs professional assistance or support.

I give the BME Mentorship Program permission to share my name, email address, and phone number with my student mentee(s).

I've read and agree to ALL of the statements above and commit to serving as a mentor next fall.

You must read all terms of the agreement.
You must agree with all terms of the mentorship program.
